Chicken Curry

One pan chicken curry to be served with rice or chips

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs/Breast
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Curry Powder
  • Garam Massala
  • Double Cream
  • Rice

Method

  1. Dice all of the vegetables and the chicken.
  2. Fry the onions until golden and add the garlic and cook for a further minute.
  3. Add the tomatos and cook until softened.
  4. Add curry powder and cook for a minute.
  5. Add chicken and cook until chicken is no longer visibly pink. Then cover and cook for another 3 minutes.
  6. Add water and continue to cook while covered for another 10 minutes.
  7. Uncover and add double cream and garam massala, cook uncovered until thickened and serve with rice.
