Breakfast Pancakes

Fluffy breakfast pancakes with fresh fruit

Ingredients

  • 200g Self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp Baking powder
  • 1 Egg
  • 300ml Milk
  • Butter
  • Blueberries
  • Strawberries
  • Maple syrup

Method

  1. Combine flour, baking powder and a pinch of salt. Mix together egg and milk. combine both mixtures.
  2. Beat in a small amount of melted butter.
  3. Heat some butter in a non-stick pan.
  4. Drop a large spoonful of pancake batter into the pan and cook on both sides until brown.
  5. Stack cooked pancakes and add fresh blueberries, strawberries and maple syrup.
